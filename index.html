About the Clinic

Premier Endocrinology & Diabetes Care in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar

Spandan Superspeciality Clinic is a premier healthcare facility dedicated to providing world-class endocrinology and diabetes care. Founded by Dr. Pratibha Pawal Aute, our clinic combines cutting-edge medical expertise with compassionate, patient-first care.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools ensuring accurate diagnosis and effective management of all endocrine disorders.

  • Led by DM-qualified endocrinologist from India's most prestigious institutions
  • Complete diabetes and hormonal disorder management under one roof
  • Personalized treatment plans tailored to each individual patient
  • Advanced diagnostic tools for precise results
